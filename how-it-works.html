How It Works:
Automatic Criminal Record Clearing

Clear your record fast in just 3 simple steps.

1

1. Check Your Eligibility

Answer a few simple questions about your past charges, convictions, and when they occurred. Our system instantly checks if you're eligible to have your record cleared.

2

2. We Generate Your Forms

If you qualify, we automatically generate the court forms you need to clear your record. We fill in the information for you to ensure everything is accurate.

3

3. We File with the Court

We file your completed forms with the appropriate court in your county. Our legal team handles all the paperwork and communication with the court on your behalf.

4

4. Get Your Record Cleared

Once the court approves, your criminal record will be sealed or expunged. You'll receive confirmation that your record has been cleared automatically.
