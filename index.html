The Record Clearing System Is Broken

Between 80 and 100 million Americans have some form of criminal record, and an estimated 50% to 60% may be eligible to seal or expunge that record. Most never do. They are not told when they qualify, the process is confusing, legal help can be expensive, and the courts do not automatically guide people through it.
